About Us

The Zenith is a non-profit organization that helps, economically backward class and transgender people who are often a target of poor services. Globally, a pandemic situation is going on due to which
whole humanity is suffering. People of every age groups
are being affected by this situation.

A lot of new problems have also arised which were never
thought to get this much limelight. The good news is
that our team have came up with a solution to all
this problems. This solution will be a major game
changer in handling the situation of pandemic.

Our Vision

We are here to serve humanity with all the resources we have.We made a research
on various field in which the pandemic has affected the people most.Our research
found out that this pandemic affected our country in three main fields-jobs,education
and health infrastructure. A lot of companies faced a major issue of funding after
lockdown due to which companies started firing employers. We provide a platform where people can
apply for jobs in companies wherethere is shortage of.People can also take freelancing
projects in each individual topics.Our country Health infrastructure in under heavy pressure.
They are now the frontline warriors fighting against Covid-19.We have came up with two
solutions for Healthcare- online appointment for doctors and a digilocker for all the
test done on patient done from which doctor can easily form a health report card.
Due to ban on all types of social gathering , school-colleges were restricted to
open and the whole education is put up on hold.
The only solution to education is providing online lectures. We have also came up
with a solution of pre recorded video lectures and q&a text box.All the video
lectures are categorized according to age groups-school,college,competition and
topic wise also.
